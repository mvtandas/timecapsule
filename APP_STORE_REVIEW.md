## What Makes TimeCapsule Unique

TimeCapsule is a location-aware memory preservation app that is fundamentally different from social media platforms:

- **Location-locked capsules:** Users must physically be at or near the location (within 5km) to open a capsule. This creates a meaningful, place-based experience that encourages real-world exploration.
- **Time-locked capsules:** Capsules can be set to open at a future date, preserving messages, photos, and videos as true time capsules that cannot be accessed until the specified date.
- **Story-style capsule viewer:** When opening a capsule, users experience the content in an immersive, full-screen story format.
- **On This Day memories:** The app surfaces capsules from past years on the same date, recreating the nostalgia of rediscovering forgotten memories.
- **Not social media:** TimeCapsule is focused on preserving memories for the future, not broadcasting them for immediate likes. The emphasis is on personal significance and place-based storytelling, not engagement metrics.

## Privacy & Permissions

- **Location:** Used to place capsules on the map and to determine proximity for opening capsules. Location is only accessed when the user interacts with map features.
- **Camera/Photos:** Used only when the user chooses to add media to a capsule.
- **Notifications:** Only requested when a user creates a time-locked capsule and opts in to be reminded when it opens. Never requested on first launch.
